About the Data

This visualization processes historical weather records parsed from CSV format. Daily readings are aggregated into monthly averages, extracting the mean high and low temperatures for each month to reveal seasonal patterns.

About Karachi

Karachi features a tropical arid climate with warm winters and extremely hot summers. Monthly temperatures range from mild 13°C lows in January to scorching 42°C highs in May and June, shaped by coastal and monsoonal influences.
